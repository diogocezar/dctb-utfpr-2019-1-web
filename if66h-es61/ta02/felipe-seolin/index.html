Graduations

Mechanical engineering

The course is focused in the areas of thermal sciences, materials and manufacturing process, applied mechanics and solids, applied thermodynamics and transport phenomena and mechanical technology. The egress will be capable of developing, designing, dimensioning and maintenance related to mechanical systems (equipment, machines, elevators); thermal systems (cold rooms, heating systems, boilers and air conditioning); manufacturing processes (foundry industries, machining, mechanical forming); and hydraulic and pneumatic systems and pumps. This allows for the development and design of new products; in the definition of manufacturing parameters; in the control of the manufacturing process and quality assurance and after-sales service. It can also work in consulting and advisory firms and in research, teaching and training institutions.

Control and Automation Engineering

The disciplinary matrices offer a deepening in specific areas as: Control and Electronic Drives, Industrial Electrical Systems, Industrial Production, Computer Systems and Software Engineering among others. In addition, they also provide materials to prepare the student to act in the management area and to encourage entrepreneurship. The egress can act in three main branches: in the Industrial area with industrial mechanical systems, control and electronic drives, computational systems and software engineering; in energy concessionaires with automation of the sectors of generation, transmission or distribution of energy; and in the service sector with building and commercial automation systems.

Electrical engineering

The electrical engineer has training in the areas of applied electricity, electronics, electrotechnology, electronics, communication and control and automation. This enables the professional to work in a wide area market, ranging from simple electrical devices to complex industrial systems. Currently, electrical engineering is one of the areas with the highest index of immediate employability. The egress will be prepared to work in industrial systems, utilities and small, medium and large companies. You can also work as a freelancer, able to design, execute and inspect technical and managerial jobs. As well as can carry out projects and conduct experiments and technological research; design, specification and analysis of electrical systems, products and processes; planning, drafting, supervision and coordination of projects and services in electrical systems; supervision in the operation and maintenance of electronic systems and equipment; assessment of safety and technical feasibility in projects; advisory, technical assistance and project consulting; elaboration of survey, expertise, evaluation, arbitration, award and technical opinion of services; and training and professional training of other electrical engineers.

Electronic engineering

The course aims to offer the student a solid and general training so that he can act in the planning, design, development and implementation of electronic and electronic systems, such as: materials, electronic and electronic control and measurement systems; electronic products and equipment; embedded systems; converters; biomedical equipment; medical informatics and telecommunications systems. The professional will be able to work with: electronic, electromechanical, magnetic, optical, instrumentation components, devices and transmitters and actuators of data, audio / video, patrimonial security and embedded electronics, measuring systems and instrumentation electronics, machine drives, electronic control and automation. In addition, it may carry out technical-economic feasibility studies, execute and supervise technical works and services, and carry out surveys, assessments and evaluations, issuing reports and opinions.

Computing Engineering

During the course, the student will receive solid training in the areas of computing and electronics. This enables high performance in the industry in the development of programs for specific applications, such as operating and network systems, design and development of hardware and software systems, embedded and intelligent systems, systems planning projects, maintenance and support to managerial tasks or consulting firms and service providers for organizations that use information and communication systems, involving evaluation and validation of the security of computer systems and communication or teaching and research. The computer engineer will be able to investigate and develop theoretical knowledge in the areas of computing and electronics and apply their knowledge in a creative, independent and innovative way. The work involves constant concern with technological updating and ability to work in groups, contributing to the search for solutions in different areas.

Software Engineering

The course aims to train professionals who work in planning, construction, management (design and quality) and maintenance of products characterized as software, to meet the current needs of society in the development of business management systems or social networking systems. Graduation requires a broad command of programming techniques and software development processes applied to the critical and efficient solution of problems involving a combination of people and computational resources, interrelating collection, storage, retrieval and distribution to data use. In this sense, Software Engineering has a set of standards, reference documents and body of knowledge that help in the creation of processes and products, as well as stimulates egress to start its own business.

Technology in Systems Analysis and Development

O curso tem como objetivo geral a formação de profissionais capazes de atuar no uso das modernas tecnologias da informação e também de se adaptarem às mudanças sociais e à evolução tecnológica, atendendo, assim, às necessidades do mercado. O aluno aprenderá a desenvolver, implantar e gerenciar sistemas para uso em processos organizacionais, passando pela infraestrutura e pela manutenção de sistemas. O tecnólogo em análise e desenvolvimento de sistemas poderá atuar como analista e programador de sistemas de informação, integrador de sistemas de informação e de soluções em tecnologias de informação, coordenador, gerente de projetos de sistemas de informação, responsável técnico pelo desenvolvimento de aplicativos e sistemas, diretor técnico, gerente em informática, consultor em tecnologias de desenvolvimento e implantação de sistemas, como profissional liberal, pesquisador ou empreendedor na área.

Mathematics

The course aims to train mathematics education professionals for basic education, with a solid scientific base in the specific area, pedagogical, human and cultural, with autonomy for continuing education. The graduates will be able to seek solutions to problems in basic training schools and thus intervene in the reality of their social environment. In addition, they may become researchers in mathematics, mathematics education, and related fields. The licensee may act in the formulation of conjectures and generalizations; in the elaboration of arguments and mathematical demonstrations, in the examination of the consequences of the use of different definitions, in the formulation of values, conceptions and beliefs, in the selection, organization and treatment of knowledge, in the establishment of pedagogical purposes, procedures and methodologies.
